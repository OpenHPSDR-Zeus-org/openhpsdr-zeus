

[Makerfabs](http://www.makerfabs.com) is currently selling the Hermes-Lite 2.0 Build9 and N2ADR companion filter board.

 * [Hermes-Lite 2.0](https://www.makerfabs.com/hermes-lite-2.html) 
 * [N2ADR Companion Filter Card](https://www.makerfabs.com/hermes-lite-2-n2adr.html)
 * [Enclosure with custom end plates](https://www.makerfabs.com/hermes-lite-2-enclosure.html)

The boards are fully assembled and undergo a screening test at makerfabs. This tests basic RX, TX and other functionality, but is not an extended burn in test. Makerfabs will handle any returns, after any problems have been diagnosed and confirmed on the [Hermes-Lite Google Group](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/hermes-lite). We typically see a return rate of less than 3%.

There is an option to add a 40mm high enclosure to your order. This comes with custom end plates for the Hermes-Lite 2.0 which are not shown in the picture. There is some assembly required to finish the unit and properly dissipate heat. See [this wiki page](https://github.com/softerhardware/Hermes-Lite2/wiki/Enclosure-and-PA-Heatsink) for details and pictures.

Payment is through the www.makerfabs.com site and appears to be PayPal or bank transfer. For safe internet purchases with credit card or bank, please use a virtual credit card number with time and amount limits. Your credit card or bank typically provides a virtual number through your online account.

[Makerfabs](www.makerfabs.com) is a company in China's Silicon Delta that specializes in small batch assembly for open source and hardware projects. They have successfully assembled 230+ N2ADR companion filter boards and 200+ Hermes-Lite 2.0 units. 

For more information on the Hermes-Lite 2.0 low-cost open-source direct down/up conversion QRP 160-10M amateur radio transceiver, please see [www.hermeslite.com](http://www.hermeslite.com) or [the wiki page](https://github.com/softerhardware/Hermes-Lite2/wiki).
